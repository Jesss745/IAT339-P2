Branding Idea

About Us

Nebula is an online cosmetic to serve as a tool for an expression of individuality, creativity, and confidence. Born from a passion for beauty and innovation, Nebula is a celestial fusion of artistry and science, offering a cosmic array of makeup products designed to empower and inspire.

Our Company to Potential Customers

At Nebula, you can embark on a cosmic journey of self-expression and empowerment where beauty knows no bounds and every product tells a story. We celebrate diversity and believe that beauty knows no bounds as inclusivity is at the heart of everything we do. We aim to help everyone can find their perfect match and unleash their inner star.

Enticing Customers to Purchase our Product

Nebula differentiate itself by offering unique and trend-setting makeup products that stand out in the market. Whether it's innovative formulations, captivating packaging, or limited-edition collections inspired by celestial themes, Nebula piques customers' curiosity and desire to try something new. Design Guidelines

Font Pairing

GT Super Ds is our brand typeface.

GT Super Ds is the primary brand typeface for NEBULA — a serif font family that The design was inspired by display types popular during the 1970s such as Trooper Roman, Times Modern and Perpetua Super (which it was named after).

Suisse Int'l is our supporting font.

Suisse Int'l is our body paragraph typeface. Its low-contrast neo-grotesque sans-serif design offers a clean, modern appearance that works well in our brand’s designs that prioritizes legibility.
